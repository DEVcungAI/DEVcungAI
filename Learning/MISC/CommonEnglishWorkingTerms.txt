· MoM (Minutes of Meeting): A summary of all meeting points, attendees, discussions, and resolutions.
· KPI (Key Performance Indicator): KPI assesses performance in achieving tasks or goals.
· MoU (Memorandum of Understanding): An informal, mutual agreement, often seen as a gentleman's agreement.
· PIC (Person in Charge): The assigned individual responsible for a task or project.
· SOP (Standard Operating Procedure): Steps or rules for executing a task.
· Follow Up: A task update or confirmation with a supervisor.
· TBD (To be Discussed): Issues pending further discussion.
· TCD (Target Completion Date): The deadline for task completion.
· TBC (To Be Confirmed): Information awaiting confirmation.
· OKR (Objective and Key Result): A performance metric based on specific objectives and key results.
